When I first heard about Bitcoin back around in 2015, mining always sounded like
the coolest and most mysterious concept tied to the protocol. Mainstream media articles trying to explain
the process of mining read something like: "bitcoin miners race to solve complex mathematical problems". This
sounded cool and all but only added to the mystery and the confusion. What is a complex a mathematical problem?
Complex for who? Were those miners trying to come out with proofs to unsolved math puzzle 🤯? So many questions...

The answer is simpler than that, and also way more boring. Miners are actually just searching for a number
from a large space of possible numbers by way of random guesses.
